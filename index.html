Math for ML · Advanced

The mathematics behind modern ML — matrix calculus, convex optimization, statistical inference, learning theory, and the math of generative models — taught concept-first, with interactive diagrams and verified code.

The spine: spectral structure reveals what a matrix does → matrix calculus differentiates it → convex optimization minimizes a loss with guarantees → inference quantifies the parameters → learning theory bounds generalization → and it all fuses in the math of generative models.
Prerequisite: the foundational Math for ML track (or equivalent comfort with vectors, matrices, gradients & probability).
